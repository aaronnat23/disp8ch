# ArXiv Paper Research

Use this skill when the task involves technical papers, research synthesis, or extracting findings from model and systems literature.

## Use when
- The user wants paper recommendations, paper comparisons, or literature summaries.
- A research-heavy task needs claims grounded in primary technical sources.
- You need to turn papers into actionable engineering notes.

## Workflow
1. Start with the exact topic, model family, or system constraint being investigated.
2. Prefer primary papers, official project pages, and reproducible benchmarks over commentary.
3. Separate factual claims, open questions, and practical implications.
4. Pull out methods, limitations, and evidence quality instead of just summarizing abstracts.
5. End with a short engineering takeaway or recommended next step.

## Deliverable
- Short list of relevant papers or sources.
- Summary of the strongest findings.
- Caveats or unresolved questions.
- Practical implication for the current task.
