# Meeting Brief

Convert raw notes or transcripts into a clear pre-read or post-meeting summary.

## Use when
- Summarizing meetings, interviews, or async planning discussions.
- Preparing a leader-friendly brief with decisions and follow-ups.

## Workflow
1. Extract goals, decisions, unresolved questions, and owners.
2. Separate confirmed decisions from speculative comments.
3. Keep quotes only when wording matters materially.
4. Group actions by owner and due date when available.
5. Produce a version suitable for fast scanning first, detail second.

## Deliverable
- Summary.
- Decisions.
- Open questions.
- Action items with owners.
