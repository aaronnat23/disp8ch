# Minimalist Skill — Premium Editorial Minimalism

Use this skill when the task calls for a refined, typographic-first, Notion/Linear-inspired interface. Produces clean, data-dense UIs with strong visual hierarchy through typography and whitespace alone.

## Use when
- Building productivity tools, documentation sites, or admin dashboards.
- The design needs to feel premium without heavy decoration.
- Notion, Linear, or editorial publication aesthetic is the goal.
- Reducing visual noise while maintaining clarity and hierarchy.

## Typography Framework
- **Sans-serif UI**: SF Pro Display, Geist Sans, or Helvetica Neue.
- **Editorial headline serif**: Lyon Text, Newsreader, or Playfair Display with tight tracking.
- **Monospace**: Geist Mono or SF Mono.
- Body text: charcoal (#111111) — never pure black. Generous line-height.
- Forbidden fonts: Inter, Roboto, Open Sans.

## Color Strategy
- Warm monochrome base with ultra-desaturated accent pastels.
- Accent pastels: pale red, blue, green, or yellow — below 30% saturation.
- Borders: `1px solid #EAEAEA` consistently.
- No large colored backgrounds.
- No gradients, neon, or heavy shadows.

## Layout Rules
- Generous padding (40–80px vertical rhythm).
- Cards: 8–12px border-radius, 24–40px padding.
- No pill-shaped containers for large elements.
- Restrict color use to 1 accent per view.
- Typographic contrast IS the primary design tool.

## Component Patterns
- **Primary buttons**: solid #111111, minimal hover (opacity shift or subtle border).
- **Accordions**: no container boxes; bottom-border separators only.
- **Tables**: borderless rows with alternating subtle backgrounds.
- **Forms**: label above, full-width input, 1px border, generous padding.

## Animation
- Subtle only: fade + translate on scroll entry, 600ms, cubic-bezier easing.
- Animate only `transform` and `opacity`.
- No auto-playing, looping, or decorative animations.

## Forbidden
- Lucide, Feather, or generic Heroicons.
- Gradients, heavy shadows, neon colors.
- Pill-shaped buttons on large elements.
- Symmetrical hero sections.

## Deliverable
- Complete editorial-quality component code.
- Strong typographic hierarchy throughout.
- Mobile-first with logical breakdown of columns.
