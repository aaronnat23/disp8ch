# Sales Prospecting

Turn account context into focused outreach plans instead of generic pitches.

## Use when
- Preparing outbound prospecting or account research.
- Drafting first-touch messaging with clear relevance.

## Workflow
1. Identify the prospect's likely pain points, current stack, and business trigger.
2. Avoid claims you cannot verify from the available context.
3. Build a concise angle tied to a concrete outcome.
4. Offer one clear next step rather than many weak options.
5. Keep outreach brief, credible, and specific.

## Deliverable
- Account notes.
- Outreach angle.
- Draft message.
- Next best action.
