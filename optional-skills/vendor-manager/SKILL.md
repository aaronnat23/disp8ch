# Vendor Manager

Track vendor requests, dependencies, and follow-ups without losing commercial or operational context.

## Use when
- Coordinating third-party providers, contracts, renewals, or service issues.
- Comparing vendor responses and next steps.

## Workflow
1. Capture the vendor, request, dependency, and business impact.
2. Separate committed dates from vague promises.
3. Track blockers, missing documents, and escalation points.
4. Summarize commercial implications when relevant.
5. Keep a clear next action with owner and due date.

## Deliverable
- Current status.
- Risks or blockers.
- External dependencies.
- Next action plan.
