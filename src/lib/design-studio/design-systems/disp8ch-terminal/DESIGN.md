# disp8ch Terminal

A dark, high-contrast operator aesthetic.

- Background near-black; surfaces a step lighter; thin cool-gray borders.
- Single red accent (`--color-accent`) used sparingly for primary actions and alerts.
- Monospace, uppercase, wide-tracked headings; sans-serif body.
- Dense, scannable layouts. Small radius (4px). Generous internal padding over margins.
- Avoid gradients and drop shadows except subtle borders.

Use `--color-*`, `--font-*`, `--space-*`, and `--radius` tokens from `tokens.css`.
